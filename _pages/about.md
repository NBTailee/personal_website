Research Interests
======
My research focuses on Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models, including:

- Self-evolving Agent, Web Agent (LLM), RF for LLM.
- Structural reasoning with LLMs
- Prompt engineering techniques
- Text classification and embedding models
- Generation-based Natural Language Inference
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

I have contributed to research papers presented at workshops associated with major
NLP conferences such as ACL, COLING, SemEval, and IberLEF.

Research and publications
======

My research investigates how prompting strategies, ensemble models, and structured
reasoning can improve the performance of language models.

Selected works include:

- Classification of Racial Stereotypes in Spanish using Ensemble Learning and BERT Adapter Heads
  (IberLEF-SEPLN Workshop)

- Text Embedding Techniques versus Generation-Based NLI for Median Judgment Classification
  (CoMeDi Shared Task – COLING)

- BERT-based and Generation-based Models with Weighted Majority Soft Voting
  (SemEval 2025 – ACL)

- Integrating Reasoning Prompt Strategies with Structural Prompt Formats for Enhanced Logical Inference
  (XLLM Workshop – ACL)
